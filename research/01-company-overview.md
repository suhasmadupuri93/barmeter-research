# Company Overview

**Name:** Barmeter Technologies (operating brand: Barmeter Steel Detailing)
**Founded:** 2021
**Industry:** AEC (Architecture / Engineering / Construction) — structural steel detailing & connection engineering services
**Site:** https://www.barmetertech.com

## What they do (plain English)
When a building is going to have a steel skeleton, the structural engineer's design drawings need to be translated into precise, fabrication-ready shop drawings — every beam, column, plate, bolt, and weld. Barmeter is that translator between design intent and the steel fabricator/erector on site.

Their pitch: reduce shop confusion, RFIs, and rework through **sequence-ready detailing** — drawings ordered the way the steel will actually be fabricated and erected.

## Mission
> Eliminate the disconnect between design and construction by transforming complex engineering & architectural intent into perfectly buildable solutions.

## Vision
A unified intelligence platform for steel construction where detailing, engineering, shop, and field coordination operate as one seamless organism.

## Core values / DNA
- Quality — converting intent into fabrication-ready solutions
- Zero-rework philosophy — rigorous QC
