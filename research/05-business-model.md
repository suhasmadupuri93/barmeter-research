# Business Model

Barmeter is a classic **services + training** play in the AEC supply chain, structured around an **onshore-sales / offshore-production** model.

## Two revenue arms

### 1. Services (the main business)
Sells steel detailing, connection design, estimating, and erection support to:
- Steel fabricators
- General contractors
- Architects / structural engineers (less common, usually via the GC or fabricator)

Pricing: project-based, "Request a Quote." Drivers are tonnage, complexity, schedule, and connection scope.

### 2. Training (the SDS/2 academy)
Authorized Nemetschek training center in Bengaluru. Trains detailers on SDS/2.
Strategic value beyond direct revenue: it's a **recruiting funnel** — best students get hired into the production team.

## Onshore / offshore split
- **US side:** sales, P.E. licensure (39+ states), client communication, code compliance
- **India side:** production (modelers, checkers in Hyderabad/Bangalore) + training

This is the standard structure for competitive AEC services firms — keeps cost-per-hour low while staying close to American clients and AISC standards.

## Differentiation claims
- "Sequence-ready" detailing → reduces RFIs and field rework
- Zero-rework philosophy
- 80% U.S. coverage on connection P.E. stamping
- Tech-driven workflow (automation in detailing)

## Where they sit in the value chain
```
Architect / Structural Engineer (design intent)
        │
        ▼
   ┌─────────────────────┐
   │   BARMETER          │  ← detailing, connection design,
   │   (translation)     │     RFIs, takeoffs
   └─────────────────────┘
        │
        ▼
Steel Fabricator (shop) → General Contractor → Erector (field)
```

## Competitive landscape (similar firms)
- Tekla service shops in India / Philippines
- US firms: Dowco, AGT Robotics' detailing arm, Eastern Steel Constructors' detailing
- Independent detailers and small fabricator-affiliated detailing teams

## Risks / opportunities for the model
- **Risk:** US construction cyclicality; AI/automation eating into low-end detailing labor
- **Opportunity:** vertical SaaS — productizing the workflow (RFI tracker, sequence visualizer) is the implied "vision" on their site
