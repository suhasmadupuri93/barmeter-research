# Technology & Standards

## Detailing & modeling
- **SDS/2** (Nemetschek) — primary detailing software; Barmeter is an authorized training center
- **Tekla Structures** — secondary modeling
- **Revit** — BIM coordination
- **AutoCAD** — 2D drafting

## Connection design
- **Descon** — connection design
- **RISA Connection** / **RISA 3D** — analysis
- AISC Steel Construction Manual — code basis

## QA / coordination
- **Bluebeam** — markup, RFI tracking
- IFC-based clash detection

## Standards
- **AISC** — American Institute of Steel Construction
- **AWS** — American Welding Society
- **OSHA** — site safety
