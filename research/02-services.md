# Services

## 1. Structural Steel Detailing
Complete shop drawings, erection drawings, CNC/KISS exports.
Deliverables include bolt summaries, gather sheets, ABM (anchor bolt material), material lists.
Workflow integrates IFC and RFI issue tracking. Goal: eliminate rework and field conflicts.

## 2. Connection Design & Engineering
- Licensed P.E.s in 39+ U.S. states
- AISC-compliant per Steel Construction Manual
- Specialties: complex frames, lateral-load systems, stairs, trusses, seismic/wind connections
- Structured RFI process — issues come back with proposed solutions
- Tools: Descon, RISA Connection, in-house spreadsheets

## 3. Estimating Support
- Material takeoffs and bid models
- Steel tonnage calculations
- Value-engineering recommendations
- Fast turnaround for competitive bidding

## 4. 3D Modeling & BIM Coordination
- Clash-free structural models built for fabrication & coordination
- Clash detection
- Erection-sequencing visualization
- Advanced geometry: stairs, ladders, rails

## 5. Connection Design Optimization
- Shear, axial, moment, bracing, truss connections
- Stair and rail design with RISA 3D modeling

## 6. Erection Planning Support
- Sequence-ready erection plans tied to 3D models
- Temporary bracing and stability guidance
- Piece-mark mapping and field bolt summaries
- On-site support during erection

## 7. SDS/2 Training
